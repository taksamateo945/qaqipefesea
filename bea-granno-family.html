All You Might Find out About Bea Granno’s Family

Bea is an Italian actress and musician that stepped into the ladder of success while filming in the HBO drama series The White Lotus.

Bea was born in the heart of Italy, Rome, and had artistic skills since childhood. She was good at music and performance, thus, attended a musical academy and finished an acting school in London. Her first TV appearance was in 2014 when she got a minor role in Don Matteo. But her first main role in Wonder When You’ll Miss Me, encouraged her to have a future bigger presence on the TV screen. 

Date of birth: May 6, 1993 

Zodiac sign: Taurus

She is known for her playing Carolina Fanti in the Doc, depicting Anna in the Zero, and, of course, all fans vote for her Mia profound role in The White Lotus.

Many of the worldwide fandom followers get curious about her family, so let’s try to figure out all possible news.
