Camila Cabello Gushes Over Her Rare And Precious Relationship With Shawn Mendes Amid Romance R

Publish date: 2024-03-05

Amid rumors they’re dating, Camila Cabello opened up about her special relationship with Shawn Mendes. During a brand new interview, the singer mentioned that she knows they are going to always be in each other’s lives and that is a great feeling to have.

Romance or not, Camila and Shawn have something truly rare and she was not afraid to make that clear.

While chatting with Clash, she sweetly said that: ‘I’ve never had many friends. I have always just had few people in my life who I trust. I feel like it is so rare to meet somebody in this industry and find a person of this quality, and I feel like Shawn’s that person for me. I trust him, and no matter the level of intensity he has around his career or which I have, he’s just normal, and that’s so rare and precious to find in this industry.’

As you know, the two artists collabed on the song Señorita and the track as well as the video present a fiery story of love, seduction and passion, showing Camila and Shawn kissing and embracing in sultry scenes quite a lot!

Since they did so much PDA, it makes sense that the romance rumors started going around as soon as the music video dropped.

Camila explained during the interview why they were so comfortable about it.

‘It’s one of the most natural things in the world for he and I to do a song together. I think I have actually known him the longest out of anyone in the music industry – we have been in each other’s lives for like four or five years, and it has been really beautiful to grow up together… It’s actually a beautiful feeling to know this person is always going to be in my life, we are always going to love each other.’
