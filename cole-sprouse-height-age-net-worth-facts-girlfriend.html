Who Is Cole Sprouse?

Cole Sprouse was born on the 4th of August 1992, in Arezzo, Italy. He is an American actor who is recognized for the portrayal of Cody Martin in The Suite Life of Zack & Cody and its spinoff The Suite Life on Deck, Cole attracted the media attention by starring on The CW television series Riverdale. He performed the role of Julian McGrath along with his Twin brother Dylan Sprouse in his first Hollywood movie, Big Daddy.
