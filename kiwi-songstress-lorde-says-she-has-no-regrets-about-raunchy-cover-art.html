Kiwi songstress Lorde says she has 'no regrets' about raunchy cover art

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Lorde shocked her fans earlier this week when she released the cheeky cover art for her new single Solar Power.

In the image, the Kiwi songstress is skipping along the beach in a tiny G-string with the camera aimed up at her derrière from underneath.

In an interview with Triple J, the 24-year-old said she had ‘no regrets’ about stripping down for the raunchy single cover.

‘It’s a specific experience thinking about everyone in your life seeing your butt,’ she said.

‘I have no regrets. I love this cover and happy for this to be how people see my butt,’ she continued.

Lorde also admitted that she’s loving the hilarious memes that have popped up online over the last few days.

‘I was sent a couple of memes. They were very funny, I liked them. I’m so out of touch with meme culture but I’m down. All the memes!’

One meme made it look like Lorde was kicking a soccer ball, while another showed her in a baby pool.
