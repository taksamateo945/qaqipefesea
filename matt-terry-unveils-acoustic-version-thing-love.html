Matt Terry Unveils Acoustic Version Of 'The Thing About Love'

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Just when we thought we couldn’t fall any more in love with ‘The Thing About Love’, Matt Terry has unveiled an acoustic version of the heart-breaking, emotional and powerful track.

Revealing the video on his Twitter, Matt defined the track as ‘a special Christmas treat’ for his legion of loyal and dedicated fans, who continue to show their unwavering support for the 2016 winner of ‘The X Factor.’

A special Christmas treat for you all, I present 'The Thing About Love' acoustic version for you lovely lot! Hope you enjoy it ?? https://t.co/peU6Wgz3j1

— Matt Terry (@MattTerry93) December 22, 2017

‘The Thing About Love’ was Matt’s second solo single, following on from ‘Sucker For You.’ The talented singer-songwriter also featured on the remix of ‘Subeme La Radio,’ which was released back in July.

His debut album ‘Trouble’ was released at the end of November, an unveiling of his hard work since coming off the show. During his time on ‘The X Factor,’ he was mentored by Judge Nicole Scherzinger, whereby he built up a rapport of phenomenal performances.

This stunning acoustic version of ‘The Thing About Love’ truly shows off his incredible vocals – we absolutely love it!
