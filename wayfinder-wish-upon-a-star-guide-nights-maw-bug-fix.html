Wayfinder Wish Upon a Star Guide: Nights Maw Bug Fix

Publish date: 2024-03-09

Are you trying to complete the Wish Upon a Star quest in Wayfinder but can’t get Night’s Maw to spawn due to a bug? Let’s examine how to fix this issue.

Even after starting the Wish Upon a Star quest and collecting all Wurm Baits, players still can’t get Night’s Maw to spawn.

In this Wish Upon a Star quest guide, I will explain how to fix the Night’s Maw spawn bug and help you get your hands on this quest’s sweet rewards.

If you’re just starting and want to get rich, check out our Wayfinder gold farm guide on how to make more money.

How to Spawn Night’s Maw – All Requirements

To spawn Night’s Maw in Wayfinder, players will need to fulfill the following requirements:

  • Be on the “Wish Upon a Star” quest – While you can fight him while not on the quest, you need to have the quest if you want to spawn the boss yourself.
  • Get enough Wurm Bait – Find Wurm Bait in the chests that you can dig up in the Highlands and place it at the pillars where Night’s Maw is located. You will need 3x Wurm Bait in total.

If you can’t place the bait, it’s a common bug and the only potential solution is to leave and come back a few times.

If you have fulfilled both requirements but still can’t get Night’s Maw to spawn, there’s one more thing to keep in mind: Night’s Maw can spawn and be fought only once every three hours per server.

Therefore, if another group of players fought this boss an hour ago, you won’t be able to make it spawn and you’ll need to wait. However, there’s no way to know how long you’ll need to wait; it could be a few minutes or two and a half hours.

Your best bet is traveling to Skylight and then returning to the Highlands a few times to see if it can be spawned. If it still can’t, I recommend doing something else and returning after an hour or two.

If you need help beating the boss, check out our Wayfinder class tier list of the best characters in the game.

Wish Upon a Star Quest Guide – Completion Rewards

After beating Night’s Maw in Wayfinder, you can expect the following rewards:

  • Glimmering Verdant Spectra
  • Gloomstone Shard
  • Vestige of Niss’ Steel

Hopefully, you managed to find your way through the bugs and can claim these rewards. Also, you can return to Seeker Avala for some extra rewards in the form of Gold.

Wrapping up

To sum up this Wish Upon a Star quest guide, players can fix the Night’s Maw spawn bug by starting the quest, collecting all Wurm Baits, and making sure nobody has spawned the boss on the same server in the last three hours.

If the boss still doesn’t spawn or you can’t place the Wurm Bait, leave the area and come back multiple times.
